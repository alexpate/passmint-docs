---

## Create a template

Login to the dashboard, and head to "templates" to create your first template. Take note of the `templateId` at the top of the page as you'll need this below.

### A note on certificates

Whilst you're getting started you can generate passes using our development certificate. However please note that in order to generate production ready passes on behalf of your customers, you will need to generate and upload your own certificate.

### Generating a pass

To generate a new pass, you will need to make a POST request to `https://api.passmint.com/v1/passes`
